My Personal Journey in Mapua University

I have been studying in Mapua for 2 years, since I was Grade 11. I have learnt a lot from this school, and I'm sure that just studying here alone has made me stronger than how I did before I studied here, and everyday in this school is a new challenge, with the activities, schoolworks, exams, etc.

I've experienced a lot of shortcomings while studying here, missed deadlines, because of how fast paced it is, hard exams, in which I had to adjust and make sure that I don't get lazy anymore, and to not procrastinate — honestly that's everything.

But despite all of that, we push through and we learn more, and because of those experiences, I'm now where I am, in this new chapter of my school life. Yes, it's harder, but it's going to be fun and an eye-opening experience for me and everyone.
